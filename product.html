Acrylic (PMMA) Sheets

Overview

Acrylic (Polymethyl Methacrylate or PMMA) is a transparent thermoplastic known for its excellent clarity, weather resistance, and versatility. It is a lightweight alternative to glass and is widely used across industries due to its impact resistance and aesthetic appeal.

Key Features

  • Exceptional optical clarity – up to 92% light transmission
  • 10x stronger than glass but much lighter in weight
  • Excellent UV and weather resistance
  • Resistant to a wide range of chemicals
  • Easy to cut, drill, engrave, and thermoform

Applications

  • Signs & Signboards
  • Display Panels & Point-of-Sale Fixtures
  • Interior Design & Furniture
  • Lighting Fixtures & Skylights
  • Industrial Guards & Machine Covers
  • Medical Devices & Enclosures
  • Automotive & Transportation Glazing

Specifications

  • Sizes : Range from 48" x 96" to 75" x 100"
  • Standard Thicknesses: Available from 2mm to 8mm in clear and in color
  • Colors Available: Clear, Opal, Black, White
  • Special Orders:Available in an array of custom colors and sizes upon request, delivering tailored solutions for every unique application.
