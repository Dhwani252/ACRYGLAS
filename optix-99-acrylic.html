Vision Acryglas® 99 Acrylic

The ultra-light matte finish of Vision Acryglas 99 minimizes unwanted glare in a variety of lighting conditions; without hindering the view of your framed artwork, graphic or photograph. Half the weight of glass, and shatter resistant, Vision Acryglas 99 is the ideal glazing choice for a variety of framing applications, as well as POP displays and directional signage.
