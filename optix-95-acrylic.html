Vision Acryglas® P95 Acrylic

Vision Acryglas P95 is a continuously processed acrylic sheet with a matte finish on one side that provides both decorative appeal and reduced surface reflections. It resists fingerprints and smudges, and is easily fabricated for use in interior applications such as partitions, doors, signage, and POP displays.

THICKNESS0.080" – 0.220"
WIDTH5" – 54"
LENGTH10" – 144"
✔ Translucent
✔ Indoor

Features & Benefits

  • Specially formulated matte acrylic finish effectively diffuses LED hot spots.
  • Provides consistent illumination for interior lighting and backlit signage.
  • Finger-print and smudge resistant surface.
  • Easy to fabricate and install.
