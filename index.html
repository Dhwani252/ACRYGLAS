Welcome to Vision Acryglas

ACRYGLAS acrylic sheets are the highest quality continuously manufactured sheets on the market today. Produced in large quantities with a proprietary innovative process, ACRYGLAS sheet products are economical as well as providing tight thickness tolerance, high optical characteristics and low stress levels. ACRYGLAS sheets are readily available in a variety of standard sizes and thicknesses, in either colorless or colors that are transparent, translucent or opaque.

Features & Benefits

Continuously Manufactured

Our sheets are extruded for consistency and cost-efficiency in every batch.

Excellent Weather Resistance

Backed by a 10-year warranty, ensuring long-term performance outdoors.

Easy to Fabricate

Low-temperature fabrication makes it ideal for custom designs and projects.

Thermoforming Advantage
