Vision Acryglas High Impact Acrylic

(Plazcryl Super)

Extruded Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) Solid Sheets, formerly branded Plazcryl, have bright clarity, excellent transparency and are easy to thermoform. These sheets provide long-life UV resistant protection for a broad range of applications. Sheets are produced according to ISO 7823-2:2003 standard.

Dimensions:

  • Thickness: 0.70 – 30.0 mm
  • Width: 1000, 1220, 1250, 2050 mm
  • Length: 600 – 6000 mm

Colors:

  • Transparent, white opaque, opal, diffusers, black, grey, green, brown, blue, red

Impact Grades:

  • S25 (R7700), S30 (R7400), S50 (R7500), S75 (R7800), S100 (R7600)

Patterns:

  • Aqua, Pinspot, Cracked Ice, NR, G-Tech, K-12

Features & Benefits

  • Highly transparent, 92% light transmission
  • 10-year limited warranty
  • Proven weather resistance, long-life UV-resistant
  • High impact resistance
