Our Foundation & Growth

Vision Acryglas is a division of Vision Polymer Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2007 in Panoli (Ankleshwar), Gujarat, by a management team of three visionary individuals. The company was formed with a mission to secure a strong presence in India’s growing plastic industry through quality, service, and customer focus. Originally starting as an injection molding unit, Vision Polymer has evolved into a comprehensive design-to-delivery solution provider. With in-house design, engineering, tooling, and production services under one roof, the company eliminates the inefficiencies of managing multiple vendors, offering streamlined solutions to its clients.

Vision Acryglas & Product Excellence

As part of its growth strategy, Vision Polymer launched a new division — Vision Acryglas — dedicated to the extrusion of PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate). This division was built to meet increasing market demand for high-quality acrylic sheets. Vision Acryglas benefits from a state-of-the-art manufacturing setup that ensures technical precision and consistent quality. ACRYGLAS extruded acrylic sheets are recognized among the best in India, offering tight thickness tolerances, excellent optical clarity, and low internal stress, making them both cost-effective and highly dependable.

Mission & Vision

Vision Polymer’s core mission is to be a market leader in its domain by supplying PMMA sheets and injection molded components to global customers. The company builds on its logistics and trading expertise to serve some of the region’s top manufacturers. Its commitment to quality, innovation, and world-class service has earned it a respected position in the plastics industry. With a solid foundation and a forward-looking approach, Vision Polymer is poised for continued success and new opportunities in the evolving polymer landscape.
