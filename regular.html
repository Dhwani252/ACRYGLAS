Vision Acryglas® Acrylic

Crystal clear, impact- and weather-resistant, Vision Acryglas is a continuously processed, high-quality acrylic sheet. High molecular weight makes Vision Acryglas ideal for almost any type of fabrication or glazing, while special UV stabilization protects the sheet from yellowing. Backed by a 10-year limited warranty, Vision Acryglas is available in a wide range of thicknesses and sizes.
