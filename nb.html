Vision Acryglas NB - Noise Barrier Acrylic

Superior Sound Blocking Acrylic

Vision Acryglas NB is a continuously-processed acrylic sheet designed to block traffic noise without obstructing views. UV stable and extremely weatherable, Vision Acryglas NB will maintain its impact strength and superior clarity through years of sunlight exposure. Vision Acryglas NB meets the requirements of ZTV-LSW 06, EN 1793 and EN 1794. Available in widths up to 100 in.
